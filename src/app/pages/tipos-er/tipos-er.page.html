Renewable Wind power
Wind energy is the energy obtained from the wind, that is to say, it is the use of the kinetic energy of the air masses.

More Solar energy
It is a renewable energy, obtained from the use of electromagnetic radiation from the Sun. The solar radiation that reaches the Earth has been used by humans since ancient times, through different technologies that have evolved.

More Hydraulic or hydroelectric energy
Hydraulic energy, hydro energy or hydro energy is that obtained from the use of kinetic energies and potentials of water currents, waterfalls or tides. Can be transformed at different scales

More Geothermal energy
Geothermal energy is a renewable energy that is obtained by harnessing the heat from the interior of the Earth which is transmitted through hot rock bodies or conduction and convection, where groundwater and rocks interact processes, giving rise to geothermal systems.

More Seawater energy
Tidal energy is the energy obtained by taking advantage of the tides: By using an alternator the system can be used to generate electricity, thus transforming tidal energy into electrical energy, a safer and more profitable form of energy.
